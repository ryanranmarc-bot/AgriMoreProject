Exploring Agriculture

Agriculture is the practice of cultivating soil, growing crops, and raising animals to meet the needs of people. This website aims to provide an overview of various aspects of agriculture, including different farming types, technological advancements, livestock management, and a historical timeline of agricultural equipment.
